# Manage Workforce

The **Manage Workforce** module ensures that only competent, authorized people
can carry out work on your asset.

> Sample content for demonstration purposes.

## Capabilities

- **Company pre-qualification** — vet contractor companies before they engage.
- **Worker competency verification** — record and validate qualifications,
  certifications, and tickets.
- **Online induction & learning** — deliver site-specific inductions and
  training, with completion tracking.
- **Real-time site access control** — grant or revoke access based on live
  competency and induction status.
- **Full visibility** — see every authorized person and what they're cleared to do.

## Typical workflow

1. [Pre-qualify a company](../guides/prequalify-a-company.md).
2. Invite the company's workers to register.
3. [Verify worker competency](../guides/worker-competency.md).
4. Assign and track online inductions.
5. [Control site access](../guides/site-access-control.md) based on status.
