# Manage Work on Site

The **Manage Work on Site** module ensures that all work carried out on your
asset is performed safely and efficiently, in a manner compliant with
legislation — across single or multiple locations.

> Sample content for demonstration purposes.

## Capabilities

- **Permit to work** — raise, approve, and close permits with full audit trail.
- **Work authorization** — confirm the right people, competencies, and controls
  are in place before work starts.
- **Multi-site coordination** — manage concurrent work across many locations
  from one place.
- **Compliance enforcement** — block work that doesn't meet the required
  controls or legislation.

## Typical workflow

1. Raise a permit for a planned activity.
2. The system checks worker competency and induction status automatically.
3. Approvers review and authorize the permit.
4. Work proceeds within the authorized window and controls.
5. The permit is closed and archived for audit.
