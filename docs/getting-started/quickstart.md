# Quickstart

This guide walks you through your first session in P2W 20:20.

> Sample content for demonstration purposes.

## 1. Sign in

Navigate to your organization's P2W 20:20 URL and sign in with the credentials
provided by your administrator. First-time users will be prompted to set up
multi-factor authentication.

## 2. Choose your asset

If your organization manages multiple sites, select the **asset** you want to
work with from the asset switcher in the top navigation. Your view, permissions,
and data are scoped to the selected asset.

## 3. Explore the dashboard

The dashboard gives you an at-a-glance view of:

- Workers currently on site
- Open permits and active work
- Outstanding risk mitigation actions
- Compliance alerts requiring attention

## 4. Complete a common task

Pick a starting point based on your role:

| Role | Start here |
| --- | --- |
| Workforce administrator | [Pre-qualify a company](../guides/prequalify-a-company.md) |
| Site supervisor | [Site access control](../guides/site-access-control.md) |
| Safety / risk lead | [Risk profiling](../guides/risk-profiling.md) |
