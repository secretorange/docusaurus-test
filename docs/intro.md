# Welcome to P2W!

**P2W 20:20** is GEMsoft7's cloud-based platform for workplace safety and workforce management. It creates a **digital boundary around your asset**, ensuring that only competent, authorized people carry out work — and that every activity on site is performed safely and in compliance with legislation.

> This is sample documentation created for demonstration purposes.

## Why P2W 20:20?

High-risk industries can't afford uncertainty about *who* is on site, *what* they're authorized to do, or *whether* the work is being carried out safely. P2W 20:20 gives operators real-time visibility and control across single or multiple locations.

- **Protect your workforce** — only competent, inducted people get access.
- **Stay compliant** — track work against the relevant regulatory frameworks.
- **Reduce operational risk** — profile, mitigate, and verify risk visually.
- **Protect your reputation** — a complete, auditable record of activity.

## Who uses it?

P2W 20:20 is trusted across **Oil & Gas, Airports, Healthcare, Facilities Management, Nuclear Construction, Pharmaceuticals, Distilleries, and Mining** — by enterprise organizations operating safety-critical assets.
