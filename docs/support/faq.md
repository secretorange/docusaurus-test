# Frequently Asked Questions

> Sample content for demonstration purposes.

### Is P2W 20:20 cloud-based?

Yes. P2W 20:20 is delivered entirely from the cloud — there's nothing to install
on your own infrastructure. See [System Requirements](../getting-started/system-requirements.md).

### Can it manage multiple sites?

Yes. You can manage work and workforce across single or multiple locations, and
switch between assets from the top navigation.

### Which industries is it designed for?

High-risk, safety-critical industries including Oil & Gas, Airports, Healthcare,
Facilities Management, Nuclear Construction, Pharmaceuticals, Distilleries, and
Mining.

### How is site access enforced?

Access is determined by live competency and induction status and can be enforced
physically through integrated turnstiles, gates, and card readers. See
[Site access control](../guides/site-access-control.md).

### Who do I contact for help?

Contact your organization's P2W 20:20 administrator, or reach the GEMsoft7
support team through your support agreement.
