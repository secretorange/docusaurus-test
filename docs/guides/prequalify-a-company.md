# Pre-qualify a Company

Pre-qualification lets you vet contractor companies before they engage with your
asset, so only suitable companies can supply workers.

> Sample content for demonstration purposes.

## Steps

1. Go to **Workforce → Companies** and select **Add company**.
2. Enter the company details and send a pre-qualification invitation.
3. The company completes the pre-qualification questionnaire and uploads
   supporting documents (insurance, accreditations, policies).
4. Review the submission and **Approve**, **Reject**, or **Request more info**.
5. Approved companies can now invite their workers to register.

## Tips

- Set an expiry on accreditations so the company is automatically prompted to
  re-submit before documents lapse.
- Use templates to standardize questionnaires across company types.
