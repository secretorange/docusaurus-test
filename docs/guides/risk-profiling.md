# Risk Profiling

Risk profiling gives you a visual picture of operational risk across your asset,
so you can act on abnormal conditions and verify the results.

> Sample content for demonstration purposes.

## Steps

1. Open **Risk → Profile** to view the current risk map for your asset.
2. Drill into a hotspot to see contributing factors and abnormal conditions.
3. Assess and score each risk.
4. Create mitigation actions and assign owners with due dates.
5. As actions complete, re-check the profile to **verify** risk has reduced.

## Reading the profile

- **Green** — within normal operating parameters.
- **Amber** — abnormal condition identified; assessment recommended.
- **Red** — high risk; mitigation action required.
