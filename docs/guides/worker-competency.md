# Verify Worker Competency

Competency verification ensures every worker holds the qualifications and
certifications required for the work they'll perform.

> Sample content for demonstration purposes.

## Steps

1. Open the worker's profile under **Workforce → Workers**.
2. Review the competencies required for their role and assigned tasks.
3. Check each uploaded certificate against its requirement.
4. Mark each competency as **Verified**, **Rejected**, or **Pending**.
5. Once all required competencies are verified, the worker becomes eligible for
   [site access](site-access-control.md).

## Expiry and re-verification

Certifications with an expiry date are tracked automatically. Workers and
administrators are notified ahead of expiry so re-verification can happen before
access is affected.
